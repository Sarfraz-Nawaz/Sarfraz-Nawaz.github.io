About Me

I work as a UX Designer in IBM's Bangalore Studio in India. Extensive experience providing software solutions from start to end.

15+ years of experience in the design business, including hands-on work as a front-end developer. I have extensive experience working in many design roles in prior businesses and am always expanding my abilities in various design responsibilities. In my experience, I've learned about user needs and behaviors in order to design interfaces that are simple, beautiful, clean, and easy to use. I am well-versed in all aspects of the design process.

I am a 40-year-old married man with two children (a 10-year-old daughter and a 5-year-old boy) who live as a joint family with my parents. I'm staying around the Auroville region, which is on the borders of Pondicherry.

I began my career as a PHP developer and afterwards worked with design tools at many companies. You may be thinking, "Why Design?" I don't have a strong justification, but I'm more connected to design and like working on it.

I feel that integrating programming and design helps me to address both end-user empathy and developers' responsibilities in order to make them intuitive on the job. So far, these combinations have shown to be more effective in the workplace.
