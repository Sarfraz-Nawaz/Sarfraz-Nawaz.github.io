DBS (iComm)

DBS is an Asian financial services conglomerate with a presence in 18 locations. DBS offers a comprehensive variety of consumer, SME, and corporate banking services. As it banks the Asian way, DBS is dedicated to developing long-term connections with clients and positively benefiting communities through supporting social entrepreneurs. With its vast network of Asian businesses and emphasis on engaging and developing its employees.

My onboarding goal is to communicate with bank workers and their supervisors to better understand the existing Intranet site and make it more engaging for their everyday operations. My responsibilities include engaging in fundamental understanding of how the Admin wishes to deliver email campaigns to their clients. So, establish Dashboard Wireframes with the inner pages listed below. After all of the wireframes are completed, the permission is upgraded to Highlevel designs. The request is to replicate the present portal's components, typefaces, color schemes, and so on. As a result, the developer may devote as little effort as possible to improving it. This project will last around 5 weeks.

My learning experience has taught me to operate quickly with their existing own design System followups that dev built.

The Challanges It's a 5-week product, and I need to work swiftly with people to gather feedback on their current site. The Business team requested that they collaborate with the existing components to create a new template. It was tough to summarize everything so fast.
