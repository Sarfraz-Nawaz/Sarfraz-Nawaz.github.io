FritoLay

About Frito Lay is a snack-industry leader that focuses on customer experiences and uses continuous innovation to drive impactful digital transformation. Modernizing e-commerce and frontline-workflow tools to delight employees and consumers. Sales Hub streamlines frontline-employee delivery routes and provides drivers and managers with an efficient mobile app to improve performance and visibility.

My onboarding begins with a meeting with FritoLay Design Managers and the Business & Teach team. My duty is to produce designs for iPhones that adhere to HFI (Human Interface Guidelines) and iPads that adhere to the Lighting design system (Saleshub). I'm a member of the UCD team. My responsibilities include creating an intuitive mobile/tablet software for frontline personnel, drivers, and supervisors. There are a few devices that worked, such as HHN SH+, which now has 40+ modules for iPhone designs. The LM is intended for managers and generally appears as a guest appearance.

My learning experience experiences have involved designing components, publishing, and comprehending how the Design System functions.

Tools: Adobe XD, Miro, Azure, Airtable.
